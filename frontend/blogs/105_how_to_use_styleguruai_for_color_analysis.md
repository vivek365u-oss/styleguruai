# How to Use StyleGuru AI for Perfect Color Analysis?

You have read dozens of articles about skin tones, undertones, seasonal palettes, and color theory. You know the rules. But every time you try to apply them yourself—staring at your wrist veins, holding white paper to your face, squinting in different lighting—you end up confused and uncertain. 

The fundamental problem with traditional, manual color analysis is that it relies on the most unreliable tool in existence: **the human eye**. Your eyes are biased by the lighting in the room, the colors of your walls, and even your emotional state. Two people can look at the exact same skin and disagree on the undertone.

This is precisely why **StyleGuru AI** exists. It replaces the subjective, error-prone human eye with objective, mathematical precision. Here is a complete guide to using the platform for a flawless color analysis.

## Step 1: Take the Perfect Photo

The quality of your analysis depends entirely on the quality of the input photo. Follow these strict rules:

1. **Use Natural Light:** Stand near a large window during daytime. Do not stand in direct, harsh sunlight (which causes heavy shadows). Face the window so that soft, even, indirect daylight bathes your face uniformly.
2. **Remove All Makeup:** Foundation, concealer, blush, and tinted moisturizer all chemically alter the visible color of your skin. Your face must be completely bare.
3. **Pull Hair Back:** If your hair falls across your cheeks, the dye or natural pigment will interfere with the skin pixel analysis. Tie your hair back or tuck it behind your ears.
4. **Neutral Background:** Stand in front of a white or light gray wall. Colored backgrounds (red walls, green curtains) will reflect onto your skin and contaminate the results.
5. **No Filters:** Submit the raw, unedited photo. Instagram filters, beauty mode, and even auto-enhance features alter the RGB values of your pixels.

## Step 2: Upload and Analyze

Navigate to [StyleGuru AI](https://www.styleguruai.in/) and upload your natural-light selfie. The AI engine will:

1. **Detect and Isolate Your Face:** Using facial mesh technology, the algorithm identifies the boundaries of your skin, separating it from your hair, lips, eyes, and background.
2. **Extract Median Skin Pixels:** The AI samples hundreds of skin pixels from the most reliable zones (cheeks, forehead, jawline) and calculates the median RGB value.
3. **Convert to CIELAB Color Space:** The algorithm translates the raw RGB into a perceptually uniform CIELAB coordinate, separating Lightness (L), Red-Green (a), and Yellow-Blue (b).
4. **Classify Your Profile:** Based on the CIELAB values, the AI determines your exact surface tone (Fair, Medium, Dusky), your undertone temperature (Warm, Cool, Neutral), and your personal contrast ratio.
