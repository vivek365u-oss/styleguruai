# Are You a Winter, Spring, Summer, or Autumn?

Discovering your seasonal color palette is the single most powerful style upgrade you can make. It permanently answers the question, "Does this color look good on me?" 

However, determining your season purely through mirror-staring is incredibly difficult because human eyes are easily biased by lighting and hair dye. To determine whether your biological canvas belongs to Winter, Spring, Summer, or Autumn, you must objectively map your genetic traits. Here is the rigorous breakdown of how to identify your season.

## 1. Step One: Determine Your Temperature (Hue)

The first step is dividing the four seasons in half by finding your Undertone.
- **Cool Undertones** (Pink, bluish-red base): You belong to **Winter** or **Summer**.
- **Warm Undertones** (Golden, yellow, peachy base): You belong to **Autumn** or **Spring**.

*Test:* Look at your veins in natural daylight. If they look distinctly blue/purple, you are Cool. If they look decidedly green/olive, you are Warm. If you look glowing in Gold jewelry, you are Warm. If you look striking in Silver, you are Cool.

## 2. Step Two: Determine Your Contrast (Chroma/Clarity)

Now you must evaluate how much contrast exists between your skin, eyes, and natural hair color.

- **High Contrast / Clear:** You have intensely dark hair against pale skin, or your eye whites are startlingly bright against deep skin. Your features are sharp and highly defined. 
- **Low Contrast / Muted:** Your hair, skin, and eyes blend softly into each other without harsh borders. You have an earthy, blended, or soft appearance.

## 3. The Final Diagnosis

Combine your Temperature and your Contrast to reveal your Season:

### The Winters (Cool + High Contrast)
If you have a cool undertone and very sharp high contrast (e.g., pale skin with jet black hair, or very deep cool skin), you are a Winter. 
**Your Power Colors:** Stark black, crisp optical white, sapphire blue, emerald green, and true ruby red. 

### The Summers (Cool + Low Contrast)
If you have a cool undertone but soft, low-contrast features (e.g., ashy blonde/brown hair, fair cool skin), you are a Summer.
**Your Power Colors:** Soft navy, slate gray, dusty rose, powder blue, and icy lavender. Pure black washes you out.

### The Autumns (Warm + Low Contrast/Muted)
If you have a warm, golden undertone and earthy, blended features (e.g., golden-brown skin, dark auburn or warm black hair), you are an Autumn. This is the most common season for Indian skin tones.
**Your Power Colors:** Mustard yellow, terracotta orange, deep olive green, espresso brown, and warm beige. 

### The Springs (Warm + High Contrast/Clear)
If you have a warm undertone but highly radiant, clear, bright features, you are a Spring.
**Your Power Colors:** Bright coral, warm peach, sunny golden yellow, and true vibrant teal.
